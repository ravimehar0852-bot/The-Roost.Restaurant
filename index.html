Our Story

A Northern Institution
Built on Great Food

Nestled in the heart of Inuvik, Northwest Territories, The Roost & Backroom Restaurant has been serving locals and travellers alike with warmth, character, and honest-to-goodness comfort food. With moose antlers on the walls and pizza in the oven, we are proudly, unapologetically northern.

Who We Are

More Than a Restaurant

The Roost Restaurant is a community gathering place. We've been feeding families, truckers, adventurers, and curious travellers who find themselves in Canada's North. Our menu spans the globe — from hand-tossed pizzas and juicy burgers to authentic Chinese cuisine and fresh seafood.

We are a licensed establishment proud to offer beer and wine alongside our food menu. Whether you're stopping in for a quick takeout or settling in for a long dinner in our cozy Backroom, we treat every guest like family.
